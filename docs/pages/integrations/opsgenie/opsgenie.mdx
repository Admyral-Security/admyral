# Connect Opsgenie

1. Go to Opsgenie.

2. Click on the **Teams** tab.

3. Click **Add Team**.

<img src="/opsgenie1.png" />

4. Create a team.

<img src="/opsgenie2.png" />

5. On the left side, click on **Integrations** and then **Add Integration**.

<img src="/opsgenie3.png" />

6. In the integrations list, search for **API** and click on it.

<img src="/opsgenie4.png" />

7. Give the integration a name (e.g. `admyral-opsgenie-integration`), select your team as **Assignee team**, and then click **Continue**.

<img src="/opsgenie5.png" />

8. Copy your API key and click on **Turn on integration**

<img src="/opsgenie6.png" />

9. Go to the **Settings** page in **Admyral**. Click on **Create New Secret** in the **Secrets** section.

10. Give your credential a name, e.g. `Opsgenie`.

11. The following secrets structure is expected:

| Key        | Value                                                     |
| ---------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| `api_key`  | Paste your **API Key** here                               |
| `instance` | `eu` or `us` - depending on where your instance is hosted |

Alternatively, you can use the following CLI command structure:

```bash
admyral secret set opsgenie_secret --value api_key=your_key_value --value instance=your_instance
```

12. Click on the **Save** button.
