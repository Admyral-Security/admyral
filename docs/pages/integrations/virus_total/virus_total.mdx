# VirusTotal

1. In **VirusTotal**, click the Settings icon in the top right corner. Next, click on **API Key**.

2. Copy the API Key.

<img src="/virustotal1.png" />

3. Go to the **Settings** page in **Admyral**. Click on **Create New Credential** in the **Credentials** section. Select **VirusTotal** from the dropdown list. Give your credential a name, e.g. `VirusTotal`. Paste the token you copied in the previous step into the **API Key** field. Click **Save Credential**.

<img src="/virustotal2.png" />
